Application Features

Bracket app enpowers its users to organize tournaments on the fly using a seamless, lightweight, and elegant app. In less than 90 seconds, you can have yourself a nice bracket.

Quick

In less than three screens, you have yourself a bracket on your mobile device. No need to boot up that clunky ThinkPad.

Interactive

Not only does our app generate the inital bracket, but enables our users to organize the tournament. You can advance players who win their respective competitions through the bracket.

Persistence

Remembering things is cool. Our application stores each bracket you create allowing you to revisit your each of your creations at your leisure.

Simple

Your Honda doesn't need a beer dispensing refrigerator. Simililarly, our app cuts straight to the chase with what you need for your tournaments and doesn't waste your time with a lot of clutter.
